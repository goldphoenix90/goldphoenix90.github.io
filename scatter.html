Scatterplot of Cities and Rides

Based on the datasets, we created the scatterplot that demonstrates how the area type may affect the average fare amount and total number of rides. We observed that total number of rides tend to be higher in urban setting than in suburban or rural areas. On average, fares are higher in rural settings than in urban and suburban areas, which may be a result of number of total rides.
